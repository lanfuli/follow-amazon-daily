# Translation Rules

Use this whenever `config.language` or `--language` is `zh` or `bilingual`.

## Chinese Mode

- Translate English source titles, summaries, and impact notes into natural simplified Chinese.
- Keep proper nouns in English when operators commonly say them that way: Amazon, Walmart, TikTok Shop, Buy with Prime, Sponsored Products, Brand Registry, Search Query Performance, PPC, Listing, ASIN, API.
- Do not leave an English paragraph as-is under a Chinese heading.
- Do not machine-translate brand names, people names, tool names, or URLs.
- Preserve links and source attribution.
- If the raw source is too thin to translate, write a Chinese editor note that explains what signal was captured and what the seller should verify.

## Bilingual Mode

- Interleave the English and Chinese versions item by item.
- Do not output all English first and all Chinese later.
- The Chinese paragraph should sound like original Chinese writing, not a literal word-by-word translation.
