# Newsletter / Analyst Signal Summary Prompt

For items in the **Newsletter / Analyst Signals** category (Billion Dollar
Sellers). Industry opinion / trend signal, NOT policy. BDS is `partial_public`:
only public archive title metadata is captured, the subscriber body is never
persisted — work from the title/excerpt only.

Per item, write three item-specific lines (translated per `config.language`):

1. **What happened** — the trend or claim the title points to, stated as a
   hypothesis ("the headline suggests…"), not as established fact.
2. **Why it matters to sellers** — if true, what it would change for selection,
   ads, margin, positioning, or growth priorities.
3. **One concrete action** — how to validate it: against official sources,
   Search Query Performance, ad reports, or category data. Not "go act on it".

Rules:
- Explicitly mark these as unverified trend hypotheses. Treat as confirmed only
  if an official source corroborates.
- The subscriber body was not captured. Do not fabricate article contents,
  quotes, or numbers from the title. If only the title is available, say so and
  give the link.
- Down-weight headline-bait phrasing; don't amplify urgency the source manufactured.
