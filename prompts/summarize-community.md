# Community Pain Signal Summary Prompt

For items in the **Community Pain Signals** category (WeAreSellers). This is a
pain-signal radar, NOT policy authority. Bodies are intentionally not persisted
for privacy — work from the `excerpt` only.

Per item, write three item-specific lines (translated per `config.language`):

1. **What happened** — the underlying pain, confusion, account-risk, or
   operational friction the thread is really about. Go past restating the title.
2. **Why it matters to sellers** — whether this looks like an isolated gripe or
   a repeated pattern worth watching; the operational area it touches.
3. **One concrete action** — log to the pain library / watch for repeats /
   verify against official sources. Do **not** recommend changing an SOP off a
   single community post.

Rules:
- Any policy or pricing claim here is unverified. Always frame it as "to confirm
  against official Amazon/Walmart sources", never as fact.
- Never reproduce the full thread text or quote it at length. Summarize the
  signal in your own words.
- If the excerpt is just a sign-up/event blurb, say so plainly and rate it low
  signal — don't dress it up.
