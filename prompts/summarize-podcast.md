# Podcast / Video Playbook Summary Prompt

For items in the **Podcast / Video Playbooks** category (Serious Sellers
Podcast, My Amazon Guy). These are media/tactics, not policy.

Per item, write three item-specific lines (translated per `config.language`):

1. **What happened** — the single sharpest takeaway in one sentence. Prefer the
   counterintuitive or specific point over generic wisdom. If a memorable line
   is in the body, you may quote it briefly.
2. **Why it matters to sellers** — what this tactic could change for ads,
   listing, conversion, inventory, or channel mix.
3. **One concrete action** — one small, testable play a seller can run this week.

Rules:
- Stand-alone. Never write "本期节目 / in this episode / the host says / 视频里讲".
  Distill the idea as if it were the speaker's philosophy.
- It's a tactic to test, not policy. Don't present podcast claims as Amazon
  policy.
- Base it on the episode `body`/`excerpt` only. If thin, say what the episode is
  about from its title and that detail wasn't captured — do not invent specifics.
- No filler, no padding. Jump straight to substance.
